What is WoundAI?

WoundAI is an advanced medical AI application designed to automatically detect, classify, and provide treatment recommendations for different types of wounds. Using state-of-the-art deep learning technology and large language models, we aim to assist healthcare professionals and individuals in identifying and treating wounds more effectively.

Our Technology

Deep Learning Model: We use EfficientNetB1, a powerful convolutional neural network, to classify wounds into 10 different categories with high accuracy.

AI Triage: Google's Gemini AI performs preliminary verification to ensure uploaded images contain actual wounds before processing.

Explainable AI: Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) visualization helps users understand which parts of the image influenced the AI's decision.

Treatment Recommendations: Gemini LLM provides detailed, clinically-backed treatment suggestions in Indonesian language.

Wound Classifications

Our system can identify and classify the following types of wounds:

  • Abrasions - Surface scrapes and scratches
  • Bruises - Contusions and hematomas
  • Burns - Thermal, chemical, or electrical burns
  • Cuts - Clean incisions and lacerations
  • Diabetic Wounds - Ulcers related to diabetes
  • Lacerations - Irregular tears in the skin
  • Normal - Non-wound skin conditions
  • Pressure Wounds - Bedsores and pressure ulcers
  • Surgical Wounds - Post-operative incisions
  • Venous Wounds - Ulcers caused by venous insufficiency

Our Mission

To provide accessible, accurate, and AI-powered wound care assistance to healthcare professionals and patients worldwide. By combining cutting-edge technology with medical expertise, we aim to improve wound care outcomes and make quality healthcare more accessible.
